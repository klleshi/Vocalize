Privacy Policy for Vocalize

Last updated: 20/09/2025

Thank you for choosing to use Vocalize, our App. Your privacy is very important to us. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what your rights are in relation to it.

By using our application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Contact Email: klleshi99@gmail.com

2. Types of Data Collected

To provide and improve our service, we collect different types of information.

  • Data Provided by You:
    • Text for speech synthesis: The text you enter, paste, or import from files (PDF) and web pages is sent to our service providers for conversion into audio. We do not store this text after the synthesis.
    • Account information (for registered users): If you choose to link your account, we collect basic information from your Google profile, such as your name, email address, and a unique identifier.
  • Data Collected Automatically:
    • Usage Data (Analytics): We collect anonymous information about how you interact with the app, such as the features you use and crashes, in order to improve the service via Google Analytics for Firebase.
    • Unique User ID: The app assigns a unique identifier (anonymous or linked to your account) to manage your plan, credits, and features.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service: To convert text to audio, save your files and audiobook projects.
  • To manage your account: To create and manage your user account (anonymous or registered) and sync your data.
  • To manage subscriptions and payments: To process your purchases for the Premium plan and free trials through the official stores.
  • To display advertising: For users of the free plan, we display ads to support the service.
  • To improve the application: We analyze usage data to understand which features are most used and to fix any issues.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses services provided by third parties to function. Each of these services has its own privacy policy.

  • Google Cloud Text-to-Speech: Used for speech synthesis. The text you provide is sent to Google to be processed.
  • Firebase (by Google): Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Google Analytics for Firebase.
  • AdMob (by Google): To display ads to free plan users.
  • Google Play Billing & Apple In-App Purchases: To securely manage subscriptions and payments. We never have access to your credit card data.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data. For any request regarding your data, you can contact us at the email address provided in this policy.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
