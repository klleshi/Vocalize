Request for Account and Data Deletion

We understand your right to data deletion and are committed to making this process simple.

How to Request Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please send an email to our support address.

Click here to send the deletion request

What Happens Next

Once we receive your request, we will proceed with the deletion of your authentication account and all personal data saved in our database (such as credits and audiobook projects) within 30 days.

You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.
